Privacy Policy

Device Storage Policy

Budget Tracker allows you to export and import it's data. In order to support this, the app needs to acces your phone's storage. The app do not collect any personal data from the device.

Camera Policy

Budget Tracker allows you to record an expense by scanning the QR code on the purchase ticket with the device camera. This feature is only supported in countries with E-Ticket systems. The app does not use the camera for any other purpose.

Internel Access

The app uses Google Play Services native libraries for supporting QR code scanning with the camera. It needs to access the Internet in order to download the native library. The native library is supported by the Google Privacy Policy. The app does not download or upload form the Internet any personal data stored on the device.
